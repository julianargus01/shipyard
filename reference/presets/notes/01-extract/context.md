# Stage 01 — Extract

## Purpose

Read the raw pasted notes and label every piece of substance. The goal is a typed inventory — nothing interpreted or reorganized yet, just classified.

## Position in flow

First stage. Receives raw pasted input directly. Output feeds `02-organize`.

## What this stage does NOT do

- Does not attach owners or dates (that's `02-organize`).
- Does not group, dedupe, or reorder (that's `02-organize`).
- Does not format into the final recap (that's `03-format`).
