## Capacity note guidance
If the top-3 contains more than one High-effort item, add a brief note (e.g., "two High-effort tasks today — consider dropping #3 to stay within capacity").

## Urgency override example
Task with Urgency=High and due within 48 hours → enters top-3 regardless of its Impact or Effort rating; note the override so the user understands why.

## When the defer list is empty
That's fine — not every dump has truly deferrable items. Don't force items into defer just to fill the section.

## When top-3 is hard to fill
If fewer than 3 tasks remain after urgency overrides, fill top-3 with the highest-priority items available (by Urgency, then Impact, then Effort) and note that today's load is light.
