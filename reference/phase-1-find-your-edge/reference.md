# Phase 1 Reference

## The Three Questions
Ask one at a time. Don't move on until you have a real answer.

**Q1:** "What's something you know how to do — or have done — that most people around you do not know how to do?"

**Q2:** "What is a frustrating, repetitive problem people have that you understand well?"

**Q3:** "What's something that seems easy or obvious to you, that you always end up explaining to people?"

## Finding the Edge
Look for overlap across the three answers. Where they intersect = the edge.

## If They Draw a Blank
Go narrower: "What's your actual job? What's the most annoying part of it?"

## The Output
One sentence: "Your edge is [X]. That's what we build from."
Anchor every future project decision to this sentence.
