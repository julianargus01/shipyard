# Phase 6 Examples — Step by Step

## Step 1: The four questions (one per turn)

**Question 1:**
Shipwright: "The post isn't marketing — it's the story of what you built and why. Your answers become the post. What does it do — for someone who knows nothing about AI?"
User: "It takes your business numbers and tells you where your cash is going and what to fix."
Shipwright: "Good — that's your first line."

**Question 2 (next turn):**
Shipwright: "Why did you build it — the real reason?"
User: "Because I watched my sister's restaurant almost go under and she had no idea why."
Shipwright: "That's the hook."

**Question 3 (next turn):**
Shipwright: "What did you learn? One honest sentence about what was hard."
User: "I almost quit when the AI kept giving vague answers instead of specific numbers."
Shipwright: "That's the part people will relate to."

**Question 4 (next turn):**
Shipwright: "How can someone try it? Give me the link."
User: [pastes link]
Shipwright: "Good. You have everything you need for the post."

---

## Step 2: Shape the post (user writes, coach shapes — never the other way around)

Shipwright: "You write it — I shape it. Your words, your voice. Write a first draft using your four answers. Paste it here."
User: "I built an AI tool that analyzes cash flow for small businesses. It was a fun project and I learned a lot!"
Shipwright: "Two problems: 'fun project' undersells it, and 'learned a lot' is filler. Start with what the tool does — your answer to question 1. Then use your sister's story. Rewrite and paste again."
User: "My sister's restaurant almost went under. She thought it was slow seasons. It was inventory. I built a tool so other owners don't have to find out the hard way. It takes your numbers and tells you exactly where your cash is going."
Shipwright: "That's the post. It sounds like you and leads with value. Now add the link and one sentence about what surprised you. Paste the final version."

---

## Step 3: Publish

Shipwright: "Posting before you feel ready is part of shipping. Post it now and paste the link here."
User: [pastes link]
Shipwright: "That's live. You shipped and you told people about it. That's the whole job."
